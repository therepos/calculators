- Objective: Determine and verify financial position of ALL
- Scope: Assets, liabilities, physical verification, supporting document review. Factual findings only, no audit opinion.

## 3. ALL's Constitution — Key Provisions

- **Para 7.1:** Supreme authority vested in General Meeting presided by President
- **Para 7.2:** AGM shall be held in July
- **Para 7.3:** EGM called by President on request of 25% voting membership or 30 members
- **Para 7.5:** Minimum 2 weeks' notice for AGM; 10 days for EGM; agenda posted 4 days before
- **Para 7.6:** No voting by proxy at General Meetings
- **Para 7.7:** AGM agenda: accounts/annual report, election of office-bearers and Honorary Auditors
- **Para 7.9:** Quorum: 25% of voting membership or 30 voting members (lesser of)
- **Para 8.1:** Committee of 10 members (President, VP, Secretary, Treasurer, 6 Ordinary Members) elected at alternate AGM
- **Para 8.2:** All office-bearers except Treasurer may be re-elected to same/related post for consecutive term. Term = 2 years.
- **Para 8.4:** Committee Meeting at least once every 4 months; 7 days' notice; President may call with 5 days' notice; half of members = quorum
- **Para 8.5:** Member absent 3 consecutive meetings without explanation deemed withdrawn
- **Para 8.7:** Committee may authorise expenditure not exceeding $10,000/month
- **Para 9.3:** Secretary keeps all records (except financial), minutes of all meetings, Register of Members
- **Para 9.4:** Treasurer keeps all funds, accounts of all transactions, authorised to spend up to $2,000/month petty expenses; cash >$2,000 deposited in bank; withdrawals signed by Treasurer AND President or VP or Secretary
- **Para 10.3:** Financial year: 1 May to 30 April
- **Para 13.7:** Shall not raise funds from public without prior written approval of Head, Licensing Division, SPF
- **Para 14:** No constitution change except at General Meeting with 2/3 consent of voting members present, and prior sanction of Registrar of Societies
- **Para 17.1:** Dissolution requires 3/5 of total voting membership consent
- **Para 17.2:** On dissolution, remaining funds donated to SPCA or approved charity

## 8. Internal Controls Findings

### Revenue, Receipts and Donations
- No SOPs
- No segregation of duties (President and Treasurer perform overlapping tasks)
- No documented approval/basis for merchandise pricing or boarding fees
- No documented cash counts
- No receipts issued for merchandise sales
- No process to record donations-in-kind
- Cash/donation box single-locked, keys held by Treasurer alone
- No collection reports or GL entries for cash receipt
- Bank-in slips not retained
- Fundraising event sales records exist (15 sample events — Pet Expo, SG Pet Fest etc.) but no reconciliation to bank deposits

### Procurement and Payments
- No SOPs
- No procurement budget
- No competitive sourcing, no justification for sole-source purchases
- No approval documentation prior to purchases
- No supplier invoices attached to payment vouchers
- No evidence of approval for payments
- No bank reconciliation ever performed
- No petty cash controls — claims without receipts, receipts submitted 309–1,044 days late
- Ineligible claims: helper meals, helper medical expenses, collagen, milk powder, earphones, mobile phone

### Governance
- **No AGMs held** — ever, per Shaw's admission. Confirmed by all 7 volunteer interviews.
- **No management committee meetings held** — no schedule, no notices, no quorum, no minutes
- **No elections** for board members
- **Only 3 of 10 required committee members** appointed
- **Treasurer (Mohan) served 10 years** vs 2-year constitutional limit (non-consecutive)
- **Constitution changed** without documented approval or General Meeting vote
- Shaw admitted preparing AGM papers with fabricated dates knowing meetings wouldn't occur
